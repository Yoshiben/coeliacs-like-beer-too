üéØ Our Commitment

Coeliacs Like Beer Too is committed to ensuring digital accessibility for people with disabilities. We are continually improving the user experience for everyone and applying the relevant accessibility standards.

We believe that everyone should have equal access to information about gluten free beer options, regardless of their abilities or the technology they use.

üìú Conformance Status

WCAG 2.1 Level AA: We aim to conform to the Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1 Level AA. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities.

Current Status: Partially conformant - Some content may not fully conform to all WCAG 2.1 Level AA standards, but we are actively working to improve compliance.

üöß Known Issues and Limitations

We are aware of the following accessibility limitations and are working to address them:

  • Interactive Maps: Some map interactions may be challenging for keyboard-only users. We provide alternative ways to access location information.
  • Third-party Content: Some external services (like map providers) may not be fully accessible. We're working with providers to improve this.
  • Dynamic Content: Some dynamically loaded content may need manual refresh for screen readers. We're improving this experience.
  • Complex Forms: Some advanced form features are being enhanced for better accessibility.

Continuous Learning: Our team regularly participates in accessibility training and stays updated with best practices and guidelines.
