🌱 Emerging UK GF Breweries

New & Growing
🔍 Local Craft Breweries

Many smaller UK breweries are beginning to experiment with gluten free options. Look out for:

  • Seasonal GF specials - Many breweries do limited runs
  • Contract brewing - Some use dedicated GF facilities
  • Alternative grain experiments - Rice, millet, sorghum based
  • Collaboration brews - Partnerships with established GF brewers

Keep an eye on these developing areas:

  • Scotland: Growing craft scene with GF experiments
  • Yorkshire: Traditional brewers adding GF lines
  • West Country: Farm breweries using alternative grains
  • London: Innovative urban breweries

🧪 Understanding GF Beer Types

✅ Certified GF

Made from naturally gluten free grains. Safest option for coeliacs.

⚗️ Gluten Removed

Made from barley/wheat but treated to break down gluten. Some coeliacs react.

🌾 Alternative Grains

Rice, millet, sorghum, buckwheat - naturally gluten free ingredients.

🏭 Dedicated Facility

Brewery only makes GF beer - no cross-contamination risk.

📊 GF Beer Market Insights

💰 Market Size & Growth

The global gluten free beer market is experiencing significant growth:

  • UK Market: Estimated £45-60 million annually
  • Growth Rate: 15-20% year-on-year
  • Consumer Base: 1% coeliac + 13% gluten sensitive population
  • Premium Pricing: 20-40% higher than regular beer

🎯 Market Opportunities

  • Underserved Market: Limited options in many areas
  • Quality Gap: Demand for better tasting GF beers
  • Venue Partnerships: Pubs want GF options for inclusivity
  • Health Trend: Growing interest beyond medical necessity
