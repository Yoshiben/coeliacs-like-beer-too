Hey, I'm Ben 👋

Coeliac • Beer Lover • App Creator

I built this app out of frustration.

After my coeliac diagnosis, I was sick of turning up at pubs only to hear "Sorry mate, just Corona" or worse - nothing at all.

So, I'm building the tool I wished existed - a proper database of where to find actual gluten-free beer. Not just the mainstream stuff, but the good craft beers we deserve!
