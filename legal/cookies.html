üç™ What Are Cookies?

Cookies are small text files that are placed on your computer or mobile device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work more efficiently and provide information to website owners.

Cookies allow us to:

  • Remember your preferences and settings
  • Improve the performance of our website
  • Provide you with relevant content and features
  • Understand how you use our website

üìã Types of Cookies We Use

üîß Essential Cookies (Always Active)

These cookies are necessary for the website to function properly. They enable core functionality such as search preferences, location settings, and basic navigation. You cannot opt out of these cookies.

  • Session cookies: Remember your current session and search preferences
  • Search preferences: Store your search type and gluten-free filter settings
  • Location consent: Remember if you've given permission to access your location
  • Map state: Remember if you have the map view enabled
üìä Analytics Cookies

We use Google Analytics to understand how visitors use our website. This helps us improve the user experience and identify popular features.

  • Google Analytics (_ga, _ga_*, _gid): Track website usage, page views, and user behavior
  • Anonymized data: IP addresses are anonymized to protect your privacy
  • No personal identification: These cookies don't identify you personally
  • Retention: Analytics data is retained for up to 26 months

üìä Google Analytics

What it does: Google Analytics helps us understand how our website is used, which pages are most popular, and how we can improve the user experience.

Data collected:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page
  • How you arrived at our website (search engines, direct links, etc.)
  • Your general location (country/region level)
  • Device and browser information
  • Anonymized IP address

Privacy protection: We have configured Google Analytics to anonymize IP addresses and respect Do Not Track signals where possible.

Opt-out: You can opt out of Google Analytics by installing the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

‚öôÔ∏è Managing Your Cookie Preferences

üåê Browser Settings

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings:

  • Chrome: Settings > Privacy and security > Cookies and other site data
  • Firefox: Settings > Privacy & Security > Cookies and Site Data
  • Safari: Preferences > Privacy > Cookies and website data
  • Edge: Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Cookies and site data
üì± Mobile Browser Settings

On mobile devices, you can usually find cookie settings in your browser's privacy or security section:

  • iOS Safari: Settings > Safari > Privacy & Security
  • Android Chrome: Chrome app > Settings > Privacy and security
  • Android Firefox: Firefox app > Settings > Privacy and security
